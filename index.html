Welcome to North Logistics Trucks Inc!

We are a trusted partner for owner-operators with 26-foot box trucks, as well as for drivers seeking employment without owning their own truck. Our mission is to provide steady loads, profitable routes, and around-the-clock support across the United States.

About Us

We specialize in professional support for drivers and owner-operators, helping them maximize their potential. With a fleet of 40 trucks and trusted brokers, we create opportunities for both truck owners and drivers looking to join a reliable company.

Our Services

  • Full Truckload (FTL) and Less Than Truckload (LTL) shipping
  • Steady lanes and trusted brokers
  • 24/7 dispatcher support
  • Full compliance: insurance, ELD, paperwork
  • Opportunities for owner-operators and drivers without trucks

Why Choose Us

  • Experienced dispatch team familiar with the market to help you find the best loads
  • Own fleet of 40 box trucks and reliable drivers
  • Operating throughout the United States - from major cities to remote areas
  • High broker ratings and excellent reputation among partners
  • Personalized approach to every driver, regardless of truck ownership
